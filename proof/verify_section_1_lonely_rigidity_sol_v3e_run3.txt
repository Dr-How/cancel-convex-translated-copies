   **Description:** **Justification Gap.**

The later counting argument only needs an arbitrary chosen lonely point for each \(r\), but **as written** this uniqueness claim is unsupported.

Because the rubric requires **zero** justification gaps, the solution is **not fully correct** under the stated grading standard.
