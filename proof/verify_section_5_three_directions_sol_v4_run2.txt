   “*then \(S=\{O,A,R,B\}\), so \(P\) is the quadrilateral \(OARB\), i.e. a parallelogram. But the theorem assumes at least three edge-direction classes, so this is impossible.*”

   **Description — Critical Error:**

So the proof is using a notion of direction class that does not match the stated definition, and the contradiction/exclusion of the parallelogram case is not valid relative to the formal statement.
