[RUN1+RUN2+RUN3] CRITICAL — Incomplete case split in Lemma lem:n3m (m=0 case)
  Location: "Case h(t')=0 ... Case h(t')>0 ... Therefore m≥1"
  The case h(t')<0 is never treated. Since t' is an arbitrary opposite-sign
  translate, the claimed contradiction for m=0 is incomplete.

[RUN1+RUN2+RUN3] CRITICAL — False claim "t_0 is the only T-element" in Lemma lem:n3m
  Location: "the term at height -1<0 vanishes (since t_0 is the only
  T-element in this degenerate case; …)"
  The proof just chose t'∈T with t'≠t_0, so t_0 is NOT the only T-element.
  The vanishing of the height-(-1) term is circular and unproved. Hence
  t'±a_1 ∈ T does not follow, and Proposition prop:n3 depends on a faulty lemma.

[RUN1+RUN3] CRITICAL — False geometric characterization of parallelograms
  Location: "P is a parallelogram iff [a_3]=[a_1] and [a_4]=[a_2], i.e.
  a_3=λa_1 for some λ>0 and a_4=νa_2 for some ν>0."
  For a CCW convex quadrilateral, opposite edges satisfy a_3=-a_1 (anti-parallel),
  not a_3=λa_1 with λ>0. The characterization is wrong, and the subsequent
  impossibility argument is built on a false foundation.

[RUN1+RUN2+RUN3] CRITICAL — Lemma lem:cross silently requires m_2≥1
  Location: "σ_T(t_2+a_2)=(-1)^1 ε_2 = -ε_2 = -(-1)^{m_1} ε_1"
  t_2+a_2 lies in F_2 only if m_2≥1. The lemma does not assume m_2≥1.
  If m_2=0, σ_T(t_2+a_2) may be 0 and the magnitude-2 contradiction collapses.
  This flaw propagates to every proposition that invokes Lemma lem:cross.

[RUN3] CRITICAL — Equation eq:sigma0 applied to t_1-a_1 ∉ T
  Location: "From k=2 at t_1-a_1 … σ_T(t_1)+σ_T(t_1-a_1)=0"
  eq:sigma0 is valid only for t∈T, but when m_1=0, Lemma lem:hzero implies
  t_1-a_1 ∉ T. Applying the equation is therefore invalid.

[RUN2+RUN3] CRITICAL — Wrong bound in Lemma lem:nogaps Sub-case B: j≥-1, not j≥0
  Location: "Note j≥0 (since h_0-1-μ>-(1+μ)>-2, and j integer ≥0)."
  From j>-2 and j∈Z, one can only conclude j≥-1. The proof then splits into
  j=0 and j≥1 only, omitting the valid case j=-1.

[RUN2+RUN3] CRITICAL — Cases γ=0 or γ'=0 not eliminated in Lemma lem:nogaps Sub-case B
  Location: "So in the surviving case: γ=-σ(t') and γ'=-σ(t'),
  σ_T(t'+a_1)=0, σ_T(t'-a_1)=0."

  The case m_1=0, m_2=1, m_3=0 is absent. For μ∉Z closure does not exclude it
