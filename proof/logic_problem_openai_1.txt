1. **The outline changes the statement by excluding cases the original conjecture still allows.**  
   Subproblem 1 says the cases \(\dim \operatorname{conv}(S)<2\) or \(|\operatorname{supp}(g_T)|=1\) are “degenerate” and should be set aside. But the original conjecture does **not** exclude them.    Subproblem 5 says to use a “common normal-fan structure implied by Subproblem 2,” but Subproblem 2 only states that **each edge direction of \(Q\)** is parallel to **some** edge direction of \(P\). That is weaker than having the same normal fan / same cyclic edge structure.

So the passage from local relations to “\(P\) must be a parallelogram” has a structural gap.
