2. **Location:**  
   “Then \(\langle\cdot,\cdot\rangle\) is positive definite and \(G\)-invariant.”

   **Description:** **Justification Gap.**

To show \(G\)-invariance, one must compute \(\langle hx,hy\rangle\) and reindex the sum using that multiplication by \(h\) permutes the finite group \(G\).
